Case Study

It Takes IO

Local co-op puzzle game built in C++/SFML where two players embody 0/1 signals to solve logic-based circuit rooms under pressure.

Pitch

Two players, two binary signals, one shared circuit to decode. Players coordinate switches, logic gates, and timing to route the expected signal to the level exit.

Core mechanics

  • Local co-op with strict communication and timing.
  • Binary signal propagation through level logic.
  • Movable logic components to reshape puzzle states.
  • Hazards forcing precise execution under pressure.

Room 1 example

Tech sheet

Type: co-op puzzle Language: C++ Library: SFML Platform: PC

Project outcomes

  • Readable logic design translated into gameplay.
  • Strong co-op dependency through communication loops.
  • Progressive puzzle design with clear testability.
