Bump Up

A VR climbing experience focused on physical effort, momentum and arm-driven locomotion.

Concept and goal

Bump Up is a VR game built around physical movement and agility. The objective is simple: reach the top of the mountain before the day/night cycle ends. The world also reacts as you climb, with weather and lighting shifting in real time to reinforce the ascent.

Gameplay and mechanics

Movement is entirely based on the player’s arms instead of continuous joystick locomotion, which helps presence and reduces motion sickness.

  • Boost: Jump by physically striking the ground with the controllers.
  • Climbing: Use the triggers to grab surfaces and pull yourself up.
  • Physical interactions: Pick up, throw and manipulate objects found along the route.

Technical challenges

  • VR locomotion: A robust physical system translating controller velocity into climbing and propulsion force.
  • Dynamic cycle: Weather and lighting synced with the player’s altitude progression.
  • Object physics: Collision, grabbing and destruction for all interactive objects in VR.
