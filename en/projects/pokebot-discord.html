Project

PokeBot Discord

JavaScript Discord bot centered on a persistent progression loop: collection, economy, upgrades, and area-based progression.

Pitch

A long-term progression experience inside Discord: catch Pokémon, run a berry farm, invest in gear, and unlock more demanding areas.

Core mechanics

  • Pokémon capture through dedicated commands.
  • Persistent economy powered by the berry farm.
  • Gear purchases that improve capture odds.
  • Progressive unlocking of tougher areas.
