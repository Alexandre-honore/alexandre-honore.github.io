Le Nectar des Etoiles

Narrative project built in 48h during WonderJam Winter 2026 (Unreal Engine), awarded 1st place.

Pitch

The player follows a worker's unusual shift aboard a tropical-production space station. Key decisions alter the narrative arc and ending.

Production context

  • 48h game jam format with strict delivery constraints.
  • Concept built from multiple imposed thematic prompts.
  • Focus on narrative impact, clarity, and replayability.

Team role

Feature implementation plus production coordination: task distribution, priorities, and keeping all disciplines aligned under jam pressure.
