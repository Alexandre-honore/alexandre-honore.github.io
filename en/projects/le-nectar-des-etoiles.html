Project

Le Nectar des Étoiles

Narrative game created in 48h on Unreal Engine during WonderJam Winter 2026, awarded 1st place at the end of the jam.

Pitch

Experience a very unusual day in the shoes of a young worker on a tropical space station that produces an ocean of exotic life.

Production context

  • 48h game jam format with strict delivery constraints.
  • Imposed themes combined into a single original concept.
  • Focus on short-form narrative, replayability, and strong atmosphere.

Team role

Feature development plus production coordination: task distribution, prioritization, and keeping a shared direction throughout the jam.
